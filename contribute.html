Contribute

Like using HungryWildcats? You can contribute to this app by doing the following!

  • Tell us about more menus or take pictures of menus! (send us an email here)
  • HungryWildcats runs on a free server on Heroku.
    Donating just $5 would help the developers A LOT and potentially move this onto an actual server, making the app much faster and smoother!
  • If you find any bugs, feel free to submit an issue to our Git Repo
