You can never decide on what to eat

And.. we're here for you!

There are a lot of places to eat in Evanston...

But there are those days when you just simply can't choose where to eat! But at the same time, you're scared to just walk into any place because you're scared that you might end up at some fancy restaurant that empties your already-too-thin wallet!

We can suggest you where to eat based on the money you have. Completely randomly. Oh, but we do tell you how much of what you can eat with that money at where!
